### Background

The Assignor has created and developed intellectual property relating to a consumer software platform for secure digital memory archiving and time-delayed delivery, marketed under the name **"Heirlooms"** (the **"Project"**). The Assignor wishes to assign all intellectual property rights in the Project to the Assignee absolutely, so that such rights are vested in and owned by the Assignee with effect from the date of this deed.

---

### Operative provisions

#### 1. Assignment

In consideration of the sum of **£1** (one pound) paid by the Assignee to the Assignor (receipt of which the Assignor acknowledges) and for other good and valuable consideration, the Assignor hereby assigns to the Assignee absolutely with full title guarantee all of the Assignor's right, title and interest in and to the **Assigned IP** (as defined in Schedule 1), to hold the same to the Assignee absolutely, together with:

(a) all rights to sue for and recover damages or other remedies in respect of past infringement of any of the Assigned IP; and

(b) the goodwill of the business connected with the use of any trade mark within the Assigned IP.

#### 2. Warranties

The Assignor warrants to the Assignee that:

(a) the Assignor is the sole legal and beneficial owner of the Assigned IP and has full power and authority to assign it;

(b) the Assigned IP is free from any encumbrance, licence, charge, or third-party claim other than as disclosed to the Assignee in writing prior to the date of this deed;

(c) to the best of the Assignor's knowledge, the Assigned IP does not infringe any intellectual property right of any third party;

(d) the Assignor has not granted any licence or other right in respect of the Assigned IP to any third party; and

(e) the creation and development of the Assigned IP was carried out by the Assignor independently, on the Assignor's own time and using the Assignor's own equipment and resources, and was not created in the course of any employment or engagement with any third party.

#### 3. Further assurance

The Assignor shall, at the Assignee's reasonable request and cost, execute such further documents and do such further acts as may be required to give full effect to the assignment under clause 1, including executing formal assignment documents for registration at the UK Intellectual Property Office, the European Union Intellectual Property Office, the United States Patent and Trademark Office, or any other relevant registry.

#### 4. Moral rights waiver

The Assignor irrevocably and unconditionally waives, to the fullest extent permitted by law, all moral rights in the works within the Assigned IP under Chapter IV of the Copyright, Designs and Patents Act 1988 and any equivalent rights in any other jurisdiction.

#### 5. Governing law

This deed and any dispute or claim arising out of or in connection with it shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the law of England and Wales. The parties irrevocably submit to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of England and Wales.

### Schedule 1 — Assigned IP

All intellectual property rights (including copyright, database rights, design rights, trade marks, patents, patent applications, trade secrets, and know-how) subsisting in or arising from the following:

#### A. Software and source code

1. **HeirloomsServer** — Kotlin/http4k backend server, including all API endpoints, authentication logic, cryptographic processing, storage management, and database schema
2. **HeirloomsApp** — Android application (Jetpack Compose), including all UI, business logic, local encryption, and device key management
3. **HeirloomsiOS** — iOS application (Swift/SwiftUI), including all UI, business logic, local encryption, and device key management
4. **HeirloomsWeb** — React/Vite web application, including all UI components, state management, and client-side encryption logic
5. All build scripts, infrastructure-as-code, deployment configuration, and CI/CD pipeline definitions relating to the Project

#### B. Cryptographic constructions and architecture

6. **E2EE envelope format** — the versioned binary envelope specification for encrypted digital assets, including the DEK-per-file encryption model, metadata schema, and envelope versioning scheme, as documented in `docs/envelope_format.md`
7. **Window capsule construction** — the combination of tlock time-lock encryption (establishing a delivery lower-bound) with Shamir secret sharing threshold deletion (establishing an upper-bound), and all implementations and derivatives thereof
8. **Device key pairing protocol** — the web-assisted persistent device keypair registration mechanism and all implementations thereof

#### C. Brand, trade marks, and domain names

9. The name and mark **"Heirlooms"** as used in connection with the Project
10. The domain name **heirlooms.digital** and any associated sub-domains
11. All logos, icons, visual identity, and design assets created for the Project

#### D. Documentation and data

12. All technical documentation, architectural specifications, research briefs, and design documents relating to the Project
13. All database structures, schemas, and data models created for the Project
14. All API specifications and interface definitions
